Who?

Sedrick Otolo is a 24 year old Co-Founder and Director at KaKebe Technologies Limited one the Leading technology company in the Northern Regions Of Uganda with Headquarters in Lira City. KaKebe Shop Online is offering biggest region online Shopping Platform in Northern Uganda owned by the youths in Lango Region. He is cutting edge in transforming his community and region with technology and impacting hundreds of lives in the region

One must be on value to be valued in the society - Sedrick Otolo

He is the Co-Founder and CEO of The Seven Seeds Uganda, a youth led organisation with the vision of building dreams and restoring hope in the lives of youths in mind blowing, inspiring and captivating ways. The organisation ventures into different programs like weekly radio shows, music, book writings, youth-child sponsorship, hosting big youth gatherings and conferences

Sedrick Otolo is also an author of two books! His first book "Being Victorious" published when he was 19 years! His second book "Building Great Character" published and launch in 2020 is very inspiring and perfect read to so many people. Sedrick believes he is a gift to the people around him as he stands out as a motivation speaker, writer, life Coach, everyday inspirations to youths and an icon in his nation. His life is Simple but Interesting and he is a firm believer in Christ!
